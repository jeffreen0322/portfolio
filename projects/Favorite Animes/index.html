Jeffrey's Favorite Animes

My top 3 animes of all time

Kara no Kyoukai

An anime movie series that explores symbolic and philosophical theme of "sense of self."

Fate/stay night: Heavens Feel Movie II: Lost Butterfly

An psychological horror anime movie that showcases the dilemma of utalitarian and selective heroism.

Monster

A mystery thriller that explores the story of a doctor who saves the life of a boy and the unintended consequences that follow.
